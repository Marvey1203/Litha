Birthday Poem for Litha

As the sun rises on this special day,
I am filled with love and admiration,
For the beautiful, intelligent, and kind woman,
Who holds the key to my heart's elation.

Your eyes, like pools of molten brown,
Sparkle with intelligence and grace,
Your hair, like smooth and flowing silk,
Gently frames your stunning face.

You are my reason for being,
My guiding light through life's storms,
I am so grateful and blessed,
To have you as my lifelong partner and friend.

On this day of your birth,
I wish you all the happiness and joy,
You deserve the very best,
My love for you will never be destroyed.

Happy birthday to the love of my life,
My beautiful, strong, and amazing girlfriend,
I cherish every moment with you,
And look forward to a lifetime of love and bliss.
